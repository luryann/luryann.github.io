My interests lie in reverse-engineering, networks, electronics, operating system development, and software development.

I'm particularly passionate about self-hosting! The concept of maintaining servers and server hardware has always fascinated me. The idea of leaving a computer running hundreds of kilometers away and talking to it through a really, really long wire is really cool. Currently, my setup hosts a diverse array of services for friends and other cool people, including public access resources such as official Linux distribution mirrors and Chrultrabook files.
