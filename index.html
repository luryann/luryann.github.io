My interests lie in maintaining something, networks, various types of electronics, and software development.

I'm particularly interested in self-hosting! The concept of maintaining servers and server hardware has always fascinated me. The idea of leaving a computer running hundreds of miles away and talking to it through a really, really long wire is really cool. Currently, my setup hosts a diverse array of services for friends and myself, such as WireShark instances, Minecraft servers, NextCloud instances, and various other miscellaneous things. And it's all done on a 2 core Celeron/4gb RAM Chromebox i got for 20 bucks.

Apart from my tech pursuits, I find joy in nature, outdoor activities, and watching sunsets. When I was younger, I used to think gaming all day was cool. As I grew older, I realized enjoying time outside is pretty cool as well. Looking ahead, I would also like to venture into the field of aviation; I've always been interested in aviation and I think it'd be awesome if I learnt how to fly someday.
